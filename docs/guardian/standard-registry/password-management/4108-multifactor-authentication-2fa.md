# Multifactor Authentication (MFA)

## Enable

To enable multifactor authentication, click **Set up** in your profile:

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (4) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Guardian shows a modal with setup instructions:

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image-1 (12).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After you enable multifactor authentication, you can download recovery codes to restore access to your account. You can copy them or download them as a `.txt` file.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image-2 (9).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Log in

Every time you log in to Guardian, the system shows a modal to verify the login attempt:

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image-3 (6).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If you lose access to your authentication app, send your recovery code to someone with database access.

## Disable

You can disable multifactor authentication by clicking **Deactivate** in your profile:

<div><figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image-5 (2).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image-4 (4).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
